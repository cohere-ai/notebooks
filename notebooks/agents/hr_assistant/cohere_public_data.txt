Cohere‚Äôs multilingual text understanding model maps text to a semantic vector space (also known as ‚Äú embeddings ‚Äù), positioning texts with a similar meaning in close proximity. This process unlocks a range of valuable use cases for multilingual settings. For example, one can map a query to this vector space during a search to locate relevant documents nearby. This often yields search results that are several times better than keyword search. To train multilingual models, you need large quantities (hundreds of millions) of suitable training pairs, like question/answer pairs.
